About Us

Devine Designs is the destination for elegant attire. Founded 20 years ago by Abigail Asante, Devine Designs has provided attire for a myriad of events from weddings, to proms, and everything in between!

On top of that, one can also bring clothing in for alterations. We are here to fulfill your every need related to clothing!

We're located at 31 Randoplh Road Silver Spring, MD 20904. We are situated in the back corner of the Colesville shopping center right next to the Giant. Come by and see us! You can also take a look at some of the work Ms Abigail has done in the gallery.
